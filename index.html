FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with GeeThree.

How long does it take to build a website?
+
Timelines vary by project complexity. A standard business website typically takes 1–2 weeks, while more complex platforms can take 4–8 weeks. We always agree on a clear timeline before we start.
Do you offer maintenance after launch?
+
Yes! We provide ongoing maintenance, updates, and support packages to keep your digital products running smoothly after launch.
How much does a website cost?
+
Pricing depends on your requirements. We offer competitive packages for every budget — from simple landing pages to full e-commerce platforms. Contact us for a free quote tailored to your needs.
Do you work with clients outside Lagos?
+
Absolutely. We serve clients across Nigeria and beyond. Our remote-first workflow means geography is never a barrier to getting great work done.
What platforms do you build on?
+
We build on whatever platform best fits your needs — custom HTML/CSS/JS, React, WordPress, Flutter for mobile, and more. We recommend the right stack for your specific goals.
How do I get started?
+
Simply reach out via our contact form or WhatsApp. We'll schedule a free discovery call to understand your goals and put together the right solution for you.
